Privacy Policy

This policy explains how CDMX Terror Studio handles local project data and authorization credentials used for content publishing.

Last updated: April 23, 2026 Operator: Luis Andres Dergal Almaraz Contact: andresdergal@protonmail.com

1. Information Processed

The workflow may process story drafts, narration audio, rendered videos, metadata files, captions, hashtags, and account authorization tokens needed to publish content on behalf of the authorized account owner.

2. How Data Is Used

Data is used to generate, render, organize, and publish content, maintain run history, and support troubleshooting of production workflows. Authorization tokens are used only to perform actions explicitly requested by the authorized user.

3. Storage

Project assets and runtime outputs are primarily stored locally on the operator's systems. Some integrations may temporarily process content through third-party services required for narration, rendering, hosting, or platform publishing.

4. Sharing

Personal data is not sold. Information may only be shared with service providers or platform APIs when necessary to complete requested publishing operations or when required by law.

5. Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect local files and authorization credentials, but no storage or transmission method is guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Your Choices

You may request deletion of locally stored authorization data and stop using the workflow at any time. Platform-side data is also subject to the policies and controls of the relevant third-party service.

7. Contact

Privacy-related questions may be sent to andresdergal@protonmail.com.
